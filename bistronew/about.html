About Black Goose Bistro

Black Goose Bistro began as a neighborhood favorite in Seekonk, MA, serving up locally inspired dishes in a welcoming setting. Founded with the belief that great food brings people together, we focus on fresh ingredients, and friendly service.

Whether you’re grabbing lunch with friends or catering a special event, we aim to make every meal feel memorable. We also take pride in our seasonal menus, showcasing the best from nearby farms.

Thank you for being a part of our story. We’re here to feed your hunger and your heart — one slice at a time.

Garlic Salmon

This is one of my favorite recipes for when folks come over for dinner. I've already shared this popular recipe with many of my friends. The best thing is it's so easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup dry sherry
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon dry mustard
  • 3-4 tablespoons freshly minced garlic
  • 1 to 2 lbs salmon filets

Instructions

  1. Mix together the first five ingredients in a shallow dish. Place cleaned and de-boned salmon filets face down in the mixture and marinate for 30 to 60 minutes.
  2. Preheat the broiler. Transfer filets to a baking sheet, skin-down. Broil on the top rack for 7 to 9 minutes or until cooked to your preference.
  3. Serve with aromatic rice and a lightly seasoned stir fry.

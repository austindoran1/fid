Linguine with Clam Sauce

This is a simple, flavorful pasta dish that’s quick enough for a weeknight and impressive enough for guests.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb linguine
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 2 cans (6.5 oz) chopped clams in juice
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook linguine according to package directions; drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and red pepper flakes, sauté for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add clams with juice and white wine. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Toss in cooked pasta, parsley, and season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.

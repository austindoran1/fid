Couscous with Peas and Mint

This light and refreshing side dish is perfect for spring and summer meals.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup couscous
  • 1 cup boiling water or vegetable broth
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup green peas (fresh or thawed from frozen)
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Place couscous in a large bowl. Add boiling water or broth, cover and let sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Fluff with a fork, then stir in olive oil, peas, mint, salt, and pepper.
  3. Serve warm or chilled as a side dish.
